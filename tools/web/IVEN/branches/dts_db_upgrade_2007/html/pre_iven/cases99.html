Processing Options for Surface Datasets:

  2. Put raw data out as is.
  3. Convert data/Limited checking/Load seperate datasets:
    • Convert [some or all of] raw datasets to QCF.
    • Do limited gross limit checking on QCF.
    • Load onto CODIAC non-HQC'd.
  4. Convert data/Limited checking/Load composited datasets:
    • Convert raw datasets to QCF.
    • Do limited gross limit checking on QCF.
    • Form composite.
    • Load onto CODIAC with limited checking.
  5. Convert data/Composite data/Perform HQC/Load composited datasets:
    • Convert raw datasets to QCF.
    • Do limited gross limit checking on QCF.
    • Form composite.
    • Perform Horizontal Quality Control on composite.
