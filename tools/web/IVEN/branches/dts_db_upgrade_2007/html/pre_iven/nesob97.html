ARM/GCIP NESOB97 (Near Surface Observation Dataset 1997)


TOI: 01 April 1997 through 31 March 1998 (Note Whole Year!)
AOI: Smaller than LSA - SW (Little Washita) (Basically, OK and KS)
  • LAT: 34 to 39 (N)
  • LON: -94.5 to -100.5(W) (WAS -78)

VERTICAL: -2 meters to 3000 meters
  • (1) Boundary Layer : < 3000 meters
  • (2) Surface Layer : 0 to 10 meters
  • (3) subsurface Layer: -2 to 0 meters


The NESOB is a custom data set designed to meet the following reqs: Land surface process studies, validation and verification of land surface processing schemes, Detailed validataion and verification of model output from regional land-atmosphere coupled models, and Models, and Derivation of surface and energy budgets. This collection of datasets should be based upon the GCIP NESOB96 datasets. See SL's ARM/NESOB web page.

***FORMING THE SURFACE COMPOSITE*** The below datasets have been converted to QCF format, checked, composited, HQC'd, then a seperate script written for NESOB was run on the HQC'd QCF to produce the "extended" QCF. The extended QCF is exactly like QCF with 3 additional parms at the end of the line (i.e.,U&V wind components, Specific Humidity). The script automatically does the computations and sets the associated QC flags. There are also some limited checkers for the extended format that were applied (like check comp/file).
