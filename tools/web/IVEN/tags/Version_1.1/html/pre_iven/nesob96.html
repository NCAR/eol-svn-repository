Hourly Surface Composite in Extended Format Description

Each line of the Hrly Sfc should also include:

  • Surface Pressure - already on each line of QCF.
  • Temperature - already on each line of QCF.
  • Specific Humidity - NEW to QCF. Must compute.
  • U and V Wind Components (m/s) - NEW QCF. Must compute from wind speed and direction.

To compute Specific Humidity do: The specific humidity is defined as the ratio of the mass of vapor in a certain volume to the total mass of air and vapor in the same volume.

e = 6.1078 * exp((17.2693882 * Td)/(237.3 + Td))
q = (0.622 * e)/(p - (0.378 * e))
where:

  • e = vapor pressure (in mb)
  • Td = dew point (in deg C)
  • p = surface pressure (in mb)
  • q = specific humidity (kg/kg). NOTE: (Data Range approx. 0.0 to 0.03 for q.)
(See Description Docs for reference.)

Sample Solutions:
Td P q
-100C 300mb 0.002073
-100C 1200mb 0.00000001
100C 1200mb 0.781172322
100C 800mb 1.536


To compute U and V Wind Components do (as from gempak):

  • U = -sin(direction) * wind_speed
  • V = -cos(direction) * wind_speed
where

direction = wind direction in degrees.
wind_speed = wind speed in meters/second.
