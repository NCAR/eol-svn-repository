The most complete version of Sid's CPC/NCEP data
should be acquired from codiac which stores the
data on hurricane at /archive/codiac/eop/katz_data.
This is the best and most complete version of his
data from 1996 through current for the daily and 
hourly gage (precip). The best grib4km data and 
preview snapshot data are also located on codiac.

Preview gif imagery are available from March 1998
to present.  Grib4km gag and mul data are available
from July 1996 to present.  Grib4km rad data are available
May 1996 to present.  Grib4km rfc data are available
April 1998 to present.  Grib4km ubr data are available
April 1997 to present.
